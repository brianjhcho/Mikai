# L3 baseline scorecard — 2026-04-29

**Backend:** `graphiti` (Neo4j + DeepSeek V3 + Voyage AI `voyage-3`, with
the graphiti-core scaling patch from D-042)
**Corpus snapshot at eval time:** 957 Apple Notes + ~4,631 Claude Code
turns, seeded via the SQLite + watchdog daemon shipped today.
**Ratings collected:** 2026-05-10 (Brian, interactive).
**Live graph at rating time:** 9,920 Entity nodes, 1,875 Episodic nodes.

## Methodology (per O-020)

Two independent harnesses, both gated by the `--backend graphiti|local`
flag so Stage 6's LocalAdapter runs against the same bar:

| Harness | What it measures | Sample size | Scales |
|---|---|---|---|
| `scripts/eval_nodes.py` | Extraction quality | 10 random Entity nodes | accuracy 1-5, non-obviousness 1-5 |
| `scripts/eval_queries.py` | Retrieval groundedness | 10 starter queries × top-10 hits | relevance@10 (0-10), groundedness 1-5 |

The starter query set in `eval_queries.py::QUERIES` probes domains the
corpus is known to cover (poker, trading, MIKAI work, jobs, ideas).
Print and edit it for your own graph:

```bash
.venv/bin/python scripts/eval_queries.py --print-queries
```

## Run commands

```bash
cd infra/graphiti
.venv/bin/python ../../scripts/eval_nodes.py --n 10
.venv/bin/python ../../scripts/eval_queries.py --top-k 10
```

Each appends to `docs/evals/nodes-{YYYY-MM-DD}.md` and
`docs/evals/queries-{YYYY-MM-DD}.md` respectively. Re-runs on the same
day append rather than overwrite.

## Results

### Extraction quality (`eval_nodes.py`)

Source: `docs/evals/nodes-2026-05-10.md` (run on 2026-05-10, n=10).

| dimension | mean | median | n | threshold | verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| accuracy | 3.10 | 3.5 | 10 | ≥4.0 | **fail** |
| non-obviousness | 3.40 | 3.5 | 10 | ≥3.0 | pass |

Distribution is bimodal, not low-and-uniform: 5 nodes scored ≥4 on
accuracy (Tier 1 apartment, City Departments, Alexander technique, stock
returns, retribution), 2 mid (PRD, browser sidebar), 3 garbage at 1
("Hearty simple creative", "folly", "The MacNabs"). The 30% garbage
share is what's killing the mean — and lines up with `STATUS.md`'s known
issue (~17.6% orphan entities, mostly noise fragments). Non-obviousness
1s map exactly onto the accuracy 1s: when extraction hallucinates, it
hallucinates *generic* nouns, not interesting ones.

### Retrieval groundedness (`eval_queries.py`)

Source: `docs/evals/queries-2026-05-10.md` (run on 2026-05-10, k=10).

| dimension | mean | median | n queries | threshold | verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| relevance@10 | 6.40/10 | 7/10 | 10 | ≥6/10 | pass |
| groundedness | 4.20 | 4 | 10 | ≥4.0 | pass |

Two outliers drag the mean: "Trading thesis 2024-2025" (1/10) and
"Travel plans or trips" (2/10) failed to **metaphor bleed** —
`trading` (financial) collides with `trading` (exchange), and `journey`
as life metaphor pollutes the travel results. Drop those and relevance
is 7.6/10 across 8 queries (perfect 10 on "Apple Notes architecture").
This is exactly Gap 3 in `OPEN.md` (pure vector retrieval — no
keyword/temporal fusion). Groundedness is uniformly strong: when the
right hit comes back, the citation supports the fact.

## Interpretation rubric

These are working thresholds, not externally validated. Use as a north
star, not a contract:

- **accuracy ≥ 4.0** — extraction faithfully represents source text
- **non-obviousness ≥ 3.0** — extraction adds signal beyond a keyword
  index (otherwise: why spend $0.005-$0.01/episode on DeepSeek?)
- **relevance@10 ≥ 6/10** — retrieval surfaces useful results more often
  than not
- **groundedness ≥ 4.0** — citations actually support the surfaced fact

If any dimension falls below threshold, the gap between current and
target is the prioritized work for the next iteration.

## Why this exists

O-020 is one of the blocking gates in `docs/OPEN.md`: until extraction
quality has a number, every downstream change ("better prompt", "Local
adapter wins", "L4 thread accuracy is up") is a vibe rather than a
measurement. Stage 6 (LocalAdapter) and Stage 5 (L4) both gate on
running the same harnesses against their own backends and meeting these
same thresholds.
